WEEKLY LAWN MAINTENANCE

We take pride in making sure every blade of grass is cut evenly each time we come. Our weekly flower bed edging keeps the lawn to garden transition looking crisp. Clients who sign up for our premium package will receive weekly gardening services to ensure the lower beds look neat throughout the entire season.

PRUNING

Proper pruning takes knowledge, the right tools, and the right time. We strive to maintain the shape and structure of your plants to ensure longevity, and to keep the gardens looking as fresh as the day they were installed.

SPRING CLEANUP

Our spring cleanups are not just the typical blow-and-go service. This is the most beneficial time to kickstart the lawn health, by de-thatching and providing the first application of turf fertilizer. Thatch is a layer of organic material that builds up between the soil and the growing blades of your grass. Up to half an inch of thatch is normal, but removing the excess material, allows for better airflow and nutrient absorption.

LEAF REMOVAL/ CLEANUP

By utilizing the most efficient equipment on the market, we are able to provide quality leaf cleanups faster, so that you have more time to enjoy your property with the shortened daylight. The timing of each cleanup varies per our client’s needs. Clients that sign with our premium package will have the property cleaned up more often, and in line with our weekly lawn maintenance.

MULCH INSTALLATION

We provide clean organic material that gives vibrant color to the flower beds and is full of nutrients to generate better soil health. By taking the time needed to do it right, we make sure the material is spread evenly and clean of clumps to give that natural look.

AERATION

Aeration is a specialty service we provide to give you that lush green lawn. Penetrating the soil and leaving small plugs of soil on the top of the surface, allows for better nutrient absorption and breaks up compaction. Aeration also allows roots to grow deeper and generates great seed-to-soil contact with the addition of new grass seed.
